A personalized blog-based web application. This application is a blog where you can create journal entries with sentient analysis, a task list and interact with various widgets such as daily pictures from NASA, Twitters top trending, a fun fact of the day and more!

My Daily Cup is your all in one widget and journal application. We created this application because we felt that users
had to login and to way too many organization and news applications to perform their daily tasks. We felt that with an
application that condenses all of your favorite organizers and widgets into one is a great way to save time and keep organized!

Refreshingly simple.
